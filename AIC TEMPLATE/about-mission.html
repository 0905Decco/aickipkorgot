Our Mission Statement

We are a Bible-reading and community church embraced in love and unity in the body of Christ. Our mission is to glorify God by making disciples, building community, and serving others with the love of Jesus Christ.

Where We Come From

Our church has a rich history of faith, growth, and community impact in Eldoret City.

1995 - Foundation

AIC Kipkorgot was established by a small group of believers who gathered in homes, united by their love for God and desire to serve the community.

2000 - First Building

Through faith and community support, we constructed our first permanent worship facility, providing a dedicated space for worship and fellowship.

2010 - Growth & Expansion

Our congregation grew significantly, leading to the establishment of multiple ministries including youth programs, women's fellowship, and community outreach.

2020 - Digital Transformation

We embraced technology to reach our community during challenging times, launching online services and digital ministry programs.

Today - Continued Growth

We continue to grow in faith, numbers, and impact, serving as a beacon of hope and love in Eldoret City and beyond.

Our Core Values

These values guide everything we do as a church community

Love

We demonstrate Christ's love through our relationships, actions, and service to others.

Unity

We are one body in Christ, working together in harmony and mutual support.

Scripture

The Bible is our foundation and guide for faith, life, and ministry.

Service

We serve God by serving others, following Christ's example of humble service.

Integrity

We live with honesty, transparency, and moral excellence in all our dealings.

Growth

We are committed to spiritual growth, learning, and continuous improvement.

Where We're Heading

Our vision for the future is rooted in faith, hope, and God's calling.

Community Center

Establish a multi-purpose community center to serve families, youth, and the broader community.

Global Missions

Expand our mission work to reach communities beyond Eldoret, sharing God's love worldwide.

Leadership Development

Invest in training and developing the next generation of church leaders and ministers.

Digital Ministry

Enhance our digital presence to reach more people with the Gospel through technology.
